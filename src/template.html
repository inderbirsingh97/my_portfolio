Projects

Gurupma Singh - PDP

For this project, my client approached me with a clear idea of what they wanted to include in the website, however, they were very unsure of the look and feel of the website. I organised a meeting with the client and discussed a few ideas that may look good in accordance to their requirements and typical designs of a blog style website. After researching potential solutions, I presented a few platforms that the client could choose from. The client was happy to proceed with Wix.com as they preferred the simplicity of the interface when it came to editing content for the blog. Although, I had not worked with Wix before, it was a challenge I was happy to tackle. It was not too different from other platforms that I had used previously (e.g. WordPress and Weebly) but the learning process was still necessary as I did not want to undermine the task.
